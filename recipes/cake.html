chocolate Cake

Description

Chocolate cake is a soft, sweet and delicious desert loved by people of all ages. It is perfect for birthdays, celebrations and special occasions.

This easy chocolate cake recipe is simple to make and has a rich chocolate falvour with a moist texture.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Vanilla essence

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients.
  2. Add eggs, milk, butter, and vanilla essence.
  3. Mix until the batter is smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool before serving.
