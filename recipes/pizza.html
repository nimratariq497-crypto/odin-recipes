Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the most popular fast foods in the world. It is made with a soft dough, tomato sauce, cheese and a veriety of delicious toppings.

This homemade pizza recipe is easy to prepare and perfect for luch, dinner or family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Chicken or pepperoni
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Olives
  • Pizza seasoning

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Sprinkle pizza seasoning.
  5. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Slice and serve hot.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy.
