Biryani

Description

Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in south Asia. It is made with fragrant rice, chicken, spices and herbs, creating a rich and delicious flavour

This recipe is simple to prepare and perfect for family dinners or special occasions. It is loved for its aroma and spicy taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions
  • Biryani Masala
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt and spices.
  3. Cook the chicken until tender.
  4. Boil the rice until half cooked.
  5. Layer the rice and chicken together.
  6. Cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy.
